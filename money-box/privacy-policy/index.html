Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KA Strategic Ventures handles information related to Money Box - Plan Purchases.

Information stored in the app

Money Box lets you create savings goals, target amounts, current saved amounts, descriptions, colors, and optional deadlines. This goal information is stored on your device and is used to show your savings progress inside the app.

Information you provide

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and the information you include in your message, such as device details, screenshots, app version, or feedback.

Diagnostics and app performance

Money Box may use third-party services, including Firebase, to help understand app crashes, diagnostics, and basic app performance. This information is used to maintain and improve the app.

How information is used

  • To provide and maintain Money Box features.
  • To save and display your goals and progress on your device.
  • To respond to support requests and user feedback.
  • To troubleshoot errors and improve app stability.

Financial information

Money Box does not ask for brokerage credentials, bank account numbers, payment card details, or login information for financial institutions. The app is a planning tool and does not move money or connect to bank accounts.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited information may be shared with service providers when needed to operate the app, provide diagnostics, respond to support requests, comply with law, or protect rights and safety.

Data retention

Goal information remains on your device unless you delete it in the app or remove the app from your device. Support emails and operational records may be kept as long as needed to respond to requests, operate the app, comply with legal obligations, and maintain business records.

Your choices

You can edit or delete goals in the app. You may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you have provided directly to us, subject to applicable law and operational requirements.

Children's privacy

Money Box is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact kastrategicventures@atomicmail.io.
