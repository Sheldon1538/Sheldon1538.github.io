Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KA Strategic Ventures handles information related to Vibe Markets.

Information you provide

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and the information you include in your message, such as device details, screenshots, app version, or feedback.

App and service information

Vibe Markets may process app activity needed to provide core features, such as market views, watchlist behavior, subscription status, diagnostics, and service performance. Some information may be processed by third-party providers that support app functionality, infrastructure, subscriptions, analytics, or market data.

How information is used

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Vibe Markets.
  • To respond to support requests and user feedback.
  • To troubleshoot errors, protect the service, and understand app performance.
  • To manage subscriptions and app functionality.

Financial information

Vibe Markets does not ask you to provide brokerage credentials, bank account numbers, or payment card details directly to KA Strategic Ventures. In-app purchases are handled through Apple.

Subscriptions

When you purchase or manage a Vibe Markets subscription through the App Store, Apple processes payment and subscription information under Apple's own terms and privacy practices. KA Strategic Ventures may receive subscription status information from Apple so the app can unlock paid features and support subscription-related requests.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers when needed to operate the app, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or complete a business transaction such as a merger or acquisition.

Data retention

Support emails and operational records may be kept as long as needed to respond to requests, operate the app, comply with legal obligations, and maintain business records.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you have provided, subject to applicable law and operational requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact kastrategicventures@atomicmail.io.
